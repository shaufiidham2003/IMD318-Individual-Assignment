My Experiences

My Name is Muhammad Shaufi Idham Bin Mohd Fadzil and I am 21 years old. I was born at Perak and currentlly staying in penang. In this page I will tell about my experiences of my life. When I was born, I was diagnosed with a disease called "Hydrocephalus". This disease is a type of disease related to the brain.

My life from a child until the age of 10 was like any other child, until I reached the age of 10 suddenly my vision gradually deteriorated. Then I went to the eye clinic and then I was referred to Penang General Hospital. After a few hours of surgery my vision was completely gone.

After I lost my sight completely, I was admitted to a Sekolah Kebangsaan Pendidikan Khas Alma (Penglihatan).
